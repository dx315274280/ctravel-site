The **Zhangjiajie glass bridge** and Avatar-style pillars are worth the trip from Shenzhen — but the journey takes planning. This guide covers the fastest realistic route.

## Getting there from Shenzhen

1. **High-speed rail** Shenzhen North → Changsha South (~3 h), then Changsha → Zhangjiajie West (~2.5 h)
2. Or fly **SZX → DYG** (Zhangjiajie Hehua Airport) when fares are low

## Park tickets & timing

Buy **Wulingyuan** area tickets in advance during peak seasons (Oct Golden Week, summer). The glass bridge has separate time slots — arrive early.

Allow **2 full days** in the park: Day 1 Tianmen Mountain / glass bridge area; Day 2 forest trails and Bailong Elevator.

## Where to stay

Stay in **Wulingyuan town** near the park gate, not downtown Zhangjiajie city, unless you prefer quieter hotels.

- [Hotels near Zhangjiajie on Booking.com](https://www.booking.com/searchresults.html?ss=Zhangjiajie&aid=AFFILIATE_BOOKING_ID)

## What to pack

- Rain jacket (mountain weather shifts fast)
- Comfortable shoes with grip
- Passport (required for park entry and hotels)
