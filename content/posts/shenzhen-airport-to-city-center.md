**Shenzhen Bao'an Airport (SZX)** is well connected — most visitors should use **Metro Line 11** instead of overpriced unlicensed taxis.

## Metro Line 11 (recommended)

- Follow signs to **Airport Station**
- Line 11 to **Futian** (~30 min) or **Nanshan** stations
- Cost ~¥7–10; machines take cash or mobile pay

## Taxi / DiDi

Official taxi queue is outside Terminal 3. Estimate:

| Destination | Time | Fare (approx) |
|-------------|------|---------------|
| Futian CBD | 35–50 min | ¥80–120 |
| Shekou | 40–60 min | ¥90–130 |
| Futian border | 40 min | ¥100 |

Use **DiDi** app with English interface after setup.

## Late arrival

Metro ends ~23:00; check last train time. DiDi or official taxi if late.

## SIM & money at airport

Telecom counters sell SIMs but **eSIM before flight** is easier. ATMs accept foreign cards at major banks — notify your bank first.
