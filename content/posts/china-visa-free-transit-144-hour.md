Many travelers use **144-hour (6-day) visa-free transit** to sample Shenzhen, Shanghai, or Beijing without a full visa. Rules are strict — read carefully.

## Basics

- Must enter through **designated ports** (e.g. Shenzhen Bao'an, Shanghai PVG/SHA, Beijing PEK/Daxing)
- Must hold **confirmed onward ticket** to a third country/region (not same country you came from)
- Stay within allowed **administrative region** (e.g. Guangdong province for some Shenzhen entries)

## Who qualifies

Nationality lists change. Check the latest **National Immigration Administration** bulletin before booking. US, UK, EU, and many Asian passports have been included in expanded policies — verify for your passport.

## At the airport

- Have printed **onward flight** itinerary
- Hotel booking helps but may not be required
- Immigration officer has final say

## When NOT to use transit visa-free

- You need to visit multiple distant provinces
- Your return home is considered "third country" incorrectly
- You plan long-term work or study

Always confirm with your airline and Chinese embassy if stakes are high.
