**Hunan** delivers China's most surreal landscapes — pillar mountains, mist, and minority culture. Foreign visitors usually center on **Zhangjiajie** with optional **Fenghuang Ancient Town**.

## How many days

| Trip | Days |
|------|------|
| Zhangjiajie core park | 2–3 |
| + Fenghuang | +1–2 |
| + Changsha food stop | +1 |

## Zhangjiajie essentials

- **Wulingyuan Scenic Area** — Avatar mountains, Bailong Elevator
- **Tianmen Mountain** — cable car, glass skywalk
- **Grand Canyon Glass Bridge** — separate ticket, time slots

## Getting to Hunan

From Shenzhen: high-speed rail via Changsha (~5–6 h total) or fly to **DYG**.

Detailed route: [Glass bridge from Shenzhen](/posts/zhangjiajie-glass-bridge-from-shenzhen/)

## Food notes

Hunan cuisine is **spicy**. Say "不要辣" (bu yao la) if you need mild. Stinky tofu is famous in Changsha — try once.

## Best seasons

- **Spring/autumn** — clearest views
- **Summer** — green but crowded and hot
- **Winter** — fewer crowds; icy paths possible

Pack layers and rain gear year-round.
