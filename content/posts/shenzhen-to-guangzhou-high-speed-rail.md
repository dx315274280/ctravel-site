**Shenzhen → Guangzhou** is one of China's busiest high-speed routes — about **30–45 minutes** station to station.

## Stations matter

| Shenzhen | Guangzhou | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Futian | Guangzhou South | Fastest for most; metro connected |
| Shenzhen North | Guangzhou South | More trains, larger station |
| Shenzhen Airport | Guangzhou South | Good if flying in |

- [Book trains on Trip.com](https://www.trip.com/trains/?AllianceId=AFFILIATE_TRIP_ID)
- [Guangzhou hotels on Booking.com](https://www.booking.com/searchresults.html?ss=Guangzhou&aid=AFFILIATE_BOOKING_ID)

## Buying tickets

1. **Trip.com / Klook** apps in English (small fee, easy)
2. **12306** official app (Chinese UI; passport registration)
3. Station counter with passport (backup)

Book 7–15 days ahead for holidays.

## At the station

- Arrive **45 min early** for first-time ID verification
- Passport + e-ticket QR
- Security like airport — liquids limits apply

## Guangzhou in one day?

Possible but tight: Canton Tower evening + Chen Clan Ancestral Hall morning. Better with an overnight.
