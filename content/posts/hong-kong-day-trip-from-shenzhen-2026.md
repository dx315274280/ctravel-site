A **Hong Kong day trip from Shenzhen** is one of the best uses of a spare day — if you understand border rules and rail timing.

## Before you go

- Check if your nationality needs a **Hong Kong entry permit** or can enter visa-free
- Carry passport; mainland ID alone is not enough for most foreigners
- Enable roaming or use a HK-compatible eSIM

## Route: Futian to Central (fastest)

1. Metro to **Futian Checkpoint** or take high-speed rail to **West Kowloon** (direct HK entry — very convenient if tickets available)
2. If walking the land border: follow signs, fill arrival card, keep receipt stub

## One-day itinerary

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning | Star Ferry + Central–Mid-Levels escalator |
| Lunch | Causeway Bay or Wan Chai dim sum |
| Afternoon | Victoria Peak (tram) or Mong Kok markets |
| Evening | Return before 22:00 to avoid border queues |

## Money

HKD cash still helps; Octopus card works everywhere. Many places take Alipay/WeChat Pay HK too.

## Return tips

Sunday evenings and public holidays = long queues at Lo Wu/Futian. **West Kowloon rail** is smoother when you book ahead.
