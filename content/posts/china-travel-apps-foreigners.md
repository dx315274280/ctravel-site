Google Maps won't save you in mainland China. Install these **before you land**.

## Essential apps

| App | Why |
|-----|-----|
| **WeChat** | Pay, chat, mini-programs for metro |
| **Alipay** | Backup payments + transit |
| **DiDi** | Ride-hailing (Chinese Uber) |
| **Baidu Maps / Amap** | Navigation in Chinese — pin your hotel |
| **Pleco** | Dictionary + camera OCR for menus |
| **Trip.com** | Trains, hotels, English support |
| **Google Translate** | Offline packs; camera mode |

## Nice to have

- **Klook** — activities and some transport bundles
- **Meituan** — food delivery (Chinese UI)
- **12306** — official rail (if you read Chinese)

## Setup order

1. eSIM data live
2. WeChat + Alipay with card linked
3. DiDi with phone verified
4. Save hotel address screenshot in Chinese
