CTravel publishes practical **English guides for foreigners visiting China**. We focus on routes we know firsthand from **Shenzhen** and **Hunan** — not generic listicles copied from travel forums.

## What we cover

- City guides (Shenzhen, Zhangjiajie, Guangzhou)
- Cross-border day trips (Hong Kong from Shenzhen)
- Practical setup (eSIM, WeChat Pay, visa-free transit)
