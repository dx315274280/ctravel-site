## Affiliate links

CTravel participates in affiliate programs including **Klook**, **Booking.com**, and **Trip.com**. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may receive a commission. Prices are the same whether you use our links or not.

## Cookies and tracking

Affiliate partners and analytics providers may set cookies to track referrals. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

## Analytics

We may use Google Search Console and privacy-friendly analytics to understand which guides help readers most. We do not sell personal data.
